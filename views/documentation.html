Introduction

unFollowIG is a free and open-source tool designed to help you compare your Instagram followers and following lists.

Unlike many other websites that ask for your password, request invasive permissions, or force you to run risky browser scripts, unFollowIG is 100% privacy-friendly. It runs entirely in your browser. No data is uploaded, saved, or shared.

You can check the source code on GitHub to verify exactly how it works.

Usage

How to Safely Get Your Instagram Data

  1. Go to the Meta Account Center.
  2. In the sidebar, select Your Information then Download Your Information.
  3. Select Instagram as the platform.
  4. Click Submit Request. Meta will notify you when ready.
  5. Download and unzip the file.
  6. Open followers.html and following.html in a web browser.
  7. Select and copy the usernames visible on the page (avoid copying extra buttons or text).
  8. Paste the followers into the left box, and the following into the right box on unFollowIG.
  9. Click Find Unfollowers to see the results.
Tip: Copy all the content as plain text from the rendered HTML page (ALT + A). This works perfectly with unFollowIG’s input format (comma or line separated).

Note: These steps are for guidance only. Instagram often changes its data export content and layout, so your experience may vary.

FAQ

  • Does this tool store my data?
    No. Everything runs locally in your browser. No data is stored or sent anywhere.
  • Does it connect to Instagram?
    No. This is a fully manual tool. You download your own data and paste it in. No login or API needed.
  • Can it show who blocked me?
    No. It only compares followers vs following. Instagram does not share block lists in the downloadable data.
  • Why am I getting empty results?
    Double-check that both boxes are filled and that you copied the usernames correctly. Also make sure you used HTML files from Meta.
  • Does it work on mobile?
    Yes, but the experience is better on desktop.
